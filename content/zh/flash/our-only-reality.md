This is Our One and Only Reality

Introubling times, some people may wonder about the idea of multiverses–where other versions of us and our reality are playing out, but perhaps differently–and whether we’re in a particularly bad iteration. The real question, writes George Musser, author and contributing editor of _Scientific American_,is not whether there are other so-called timelines: according to quantum physics, there almost certainly are.
